# Air

Air is a free and easy-to-use secure messaging app.

- Send end-to-end encrypted texts, photos, videos, and documents.
- Create group chats with friends and family. Feel free to bring your coworkers too.
- Air doesn’t collect sensitive information like your phone number or email address, and doesn’t retain metadata, like who you talk to and when.
- Air is open-source, and is based on the Messaging Layer Security (MLS) protocol and other open standards.
- Air is designed, built, and hosted in the European Union (EU).
- Air is not backed by investors or advertisers.

Air is regularly updated with new features and improvements.

> **It’s still early days.** Air is built on a solid foundation. Its underlying security protocol implementation recently undergone an [independent security audit](https://blog.phnx.im/openmls-independent-security-audit/). However, the app is still evolving. It is not yet full-featured and not all advanced security measures have been implemented.
>

## Install

> **Air is currently invite-only.** To sign up on Air you need an invite code from an existing Air user.
>

The Air app is available for both Android and iOS, with desktop support coming soon. You can find links to the Google Play and Apple AppStore here: [https://air.ms/install](https://air.ms/install)
